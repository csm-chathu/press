Core Operations

Quotation templates — save common job types (business cards, brochures, banners) for 1-click re-quoting
Job card cloning — duplicate a completed job card for repeat orders
Rush/priority jobs — mark jobs urgent with a visual flag and push them to the top of the queue
Client proof approval — let portal clients approve or reject artwork directly from their portal (not via phone)
Artwork file uploads — attach and version-control design files per job card
Communication

Email notifications — automated emails on job milestones alongside SMS (Laravel Mail + queues)
WhatsApp Business API — send proof images and status updates via WhatsApp instead of plain SMS
In-app notification bell — real-time notifications when jobs are assigned, overdue, or approved
Customer statement emails — auto-send monthly outstanding balance statements
Scheduling & Planning

Machine scheduler / calendar — drag-and-drop visual calendar to book machines per job
Operator workload view — see how many jobs each operator has in their queue
Production capacity planning — flag when a machine is overbooked for a given day
Due date alerts — automatic warning when a job is approaching or past due
Finance & Reporting

Customer statements — aged receivables, credit balance, payment history per customer
Supplier statements — outstanding AP, payment history per supplier
VAT/tax reports — monthly tax summary ready for filing
Commission tracking — calculate sales staff commission from converted quotations
Excel/CSV export — export any table (jobs, invoices, costing) to spreadsheet
P&L report — monthly/yearly profit and loss from actual job costing data
Inventory & Purchasing

Stock reorder alerts + auto PO — generate a draft purchase order when stock hits reorder level
Supplier price comparison — track same material from multiple suppliers and pick cheapest
Purchase requisition workflow — staff requests → manager approves → PO issued
Barcode scanning — scan product barcodes during GRN to auto-fill line items
HR & Staff

Employee management — basic HR profiles, department, contact info
Attendance tracking — daily clock-in/out linked to labour cost
Payroll/salary slips — generate monthly salary slips per employee
Machine maintenance log — schedule and record maintenance per press machine
Customer & Portal

Customer credit limits — block new orders if balance exceeds credit limit
Customer loyalty/tiered pricing — auto-apply discounts for high-volume clients
Portal file downloads — let clients download their own invoices and job card PDFs
Delivery tracking page — real-time public tracking link sent with delivery SMS
System & Security

Role-based dashboard customisation — operators see machine queue; managers see revenue; accountants see finance KPIs
Two-factor authentication — TOTP 2FA for admin/owner accounts
Full audit trail — who changed what and when on every record
Data backup & restore — scheduled DB dump with restore from admin panel
PWA / mobile app — installable mobile-optimised view for production floor tablet use
Webhook integrations — POST to external URL when job status changes (connect to Zapier, etc.)
The highest-value ones to build next (based on a typical print shop workflow):

Quotation templates — saves the most daily time
Email notifications — reaches clients who don't respond to SMS
Machine scheduler — prevents double-booking machines
Customer statements — reduces AR chasing time
Client proof approval via portal — eliminates back-and-forth calls
